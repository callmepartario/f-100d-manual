
# Engine Shutdown

1. Brakes—**HOLD**
2. Standby Instrument Inverter Switch—**ON**
3. Throttle—**72%**—at least 30 seconds.
4. Speed Brake Switch—**AS DESIRED**
5. Nose gear ground safety pin—**INSTALLED**
6. Throttle—**OFF**
7. Engine Master Switch—**OFF**
8. Standby Inverter—**OFF**
9. Battery Switch—**OFF**
10. Control stick—**ROTATE**—Bleed flight control hydraulic system pressure.
11. Landing gear doors—**OPEN**—Pull the landing gear emergency lowering handle to open landing gear wheel well doors.
