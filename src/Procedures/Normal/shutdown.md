
## Engine Shutdown
1. Brakes - Hold.
2. Standby inverter - ON.
3. Throttle - 72% for at least 30 seconds.
4. Speed brake switch - As desired.
5. Nose gear ground safety pin - Installed.
6. Throttel - OFF.
7. Engine master switch - OFF.
8. Standby inverter - OFF.
9. Battery switch - OFF.
10. Control stick - Rotate. To bleed off flight control hydraulic system pressure.
11. Landing gear doors - Open. Pull landing gear emergency lowering handle to open landing gear wheel well doors.
