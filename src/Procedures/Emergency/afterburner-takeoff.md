## Afterburner Failure During Takeoff
1. THROTTLE — INBOARD. Immediately move throttle
inboard out of AFTERBURNER range to MILITARY to
ensure exhaust nozzle closing.

    !!! warning
        If afterburner is used and light up is not achieved, considerably less than military thrust will be output. Return the throttle inboard immediately to regain militay thrust.

2. EXTERNAL LOAD — JETTISON (if necessary), If
the airplane starts to decelerate or altitude cannot be maintained,
it must be assumed that either the exhaust nozzle
failed to close or that the gross weight is too great to continue.
In either case, jettison external loads to lighten
airplane gross weight.

    !!! note
        In most cases, if external loads are jettisoned
        at the time of afterburner failure,
        the takeoff can be successfully completed
        in Military Thrust.
