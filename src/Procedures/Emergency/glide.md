
## Recommended Glide
** GRAPHIC * 3-53

!!! note
    Glide distance with gear and flaps up, speed
    brake in, approximately 18 nautical miles
    per 10,000 feet descent.

!!! note

    one-half glide distance of clean
    airplane.

!!! note

    from altitude to touchdown.
