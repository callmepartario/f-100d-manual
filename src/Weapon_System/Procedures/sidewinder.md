# Sidewinder

1. [Missile Master](../aim-9-sidewinder.md#missile-master) - READY
2. [Gun Missile Switch](../armament-control-panels.md#gun-missile-switch) - MISSILES
3. Check Missile Status Light is Green (if not use [Station Bypass](../aim-9-sidewinder.md#station-bypass) to step until a working missile can be found, trigger can also be used to step but runs the risk of inadvertently firing a sidewinder). Tone should be heard.
4. Listen for higher pitched tone indicating a valid heat source and depress second stage trigger to fire the missile.
